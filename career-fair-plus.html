SETUP GUIDE

Three Steps to Get Set Up

1

Download the free Career Fair Plus app

Career Fair Plus is available on both iOS and Android. It's the official app used by hundreds of engineering career fairs nationwide to help students search employers, navigate the floor, and bookmark companies of interest.

2

Select "Student/Candidate" then search for your school

When you open the app, choose Student/Candidate as your role. On the "Select an Organization" screen, search for University of Michigan Engineering and select it.

FINALLY
3

Select "Fall Engineering Career Fair hosted by SWE/TBP"

From the list of University of Michigan events, tap Fall Engineering Career Fair hosted by SWE/TBP. You're in — browse employers, view floor maps, and save companies to your list.
