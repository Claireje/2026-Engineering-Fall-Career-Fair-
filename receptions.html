WHAT TO EXPECT

Guidelines & Dress Code

These events are designed to encourage casual, open conversation. Please review the guidelines for your role below.

For Students

No Resumes

Leave your resume at home. Receptions are intentionally resume-free to encourage genuine, low-pressure conversation with recruiters.

Business Casual Attire

Dress professionally but comfortably — business casual is recommended to maintain a polished yet relaxed atmosphere.

RSVP Required

You must RSVP to attend. This helps organizers manage layout and refreshments. RSVP deadline is August 29th, 2026.

Attend the Right Night

Sunday's reception is for companies attending the Monday Career Fair. Monday's reception is for companies attending the Tuesday Career Fair. Check the company list to plan accordingly.

For Company Representatives

No Resume Collection

Students have been instructed not to bring resumes. This event is intentionally informal — focus on open conversation rather than formal recruiting.

Wear Branded Apparel

Company representatives are encouraged to wear branded t-shirts or easily identifiable attire for easy recognition and a welcoming, approachable vibe.

Attend Your Assigned Night

Your assigned night corresponds to your Career Fair day. If you're attending the Monday fair, come to the Sunday reception; Tuesday fair companies attend the Monday reception.

Engage Openly

These events draw 4,000+ students annually. Use the relaxed setting to share your company culture, role opportunities, and team stories without the formality of a booth.
