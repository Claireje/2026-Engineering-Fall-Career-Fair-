Receptions & SWE/TBP Events

What are employer receptions?

Employer receptions are relaxed pre-fair networking events hosted before the Career Fair. They help companies connect with students in a more casual, conversational setting.

Which reception night should my company attend?

Reception nights correspond to Career Fair attendance days. Companies attending the Monday fair attend the Sunday reception, and companies attending the Tuesday fair attend the Monday reception. View reception details →

Can companies participate in other SWE/TBP events?

Yes. Employers may have opportunities to participate in receptions, sponsorships, and other SWE/TBP-related events connected to the Career Fair experience.
