HOW IT WORKS

EGL Coat Check

Heading to the Career Fair? Drop off your coats, backpacks, and other items at Engineering Global Leadership's (EGL) FREE Coat & Bag Check in the Pierpont East Room. Just bring your claim ticket to pick them up at any time during the event — simple and stress-free!

Drop Off
Leave your coat, bag, or backpack at the Pierpont East Room before entering the fair.
Get Your Ticket
You'll receive a claim ticket — keep it safe to retrieve your items anytime.
Pick Up Anytime
Return to the Pierpont East Room with your ticket at any point during the event.

POLICIES

Lost & Found

EGL Safekeeping

EGL will ensure the safekeeping, proper handling, and proper storage of all items checked during the event. The University will be responsible in the case of lost items.

Unclaimed Items

For personal items left behind after the fair ends, the Engineering Career Resource Center (ECRC) will handle storage and return. The ECRC will store items securely and attempt to contact the owner if identifiable information is available.

QUESTIONS?

Get in Touch

For questions about unclaimed items or the coat check service, contact the ECRC or the Career Fair organizing team.
