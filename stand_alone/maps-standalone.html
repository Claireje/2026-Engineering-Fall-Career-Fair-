TRANSPORTATION

Getting There

North Campus is easily accessible by bus, bike, car, or on foot. We recommend taking the campus bus to avoid parking constraints on fair days.

Campus Bus

The Northwood/Bursley-Baits routes connect Central Campus to North Campus every 5–15 minutes. Drop off near Chrysler Center. Free with M-Card.

View routes
Parking

NC5 Surface Lot and the Bonisteel parking structure are closest to Chrysler Center. Visitor permits available at lot kiosks.

Visitor parking info
Walking & Biking

About a 25-minute walk from Central Campus via Fuller Rd. Bike racks available outside Chrysler Center and Pierpont Commons.

Walking directions
Rideshare

Uber and Lyft drop-off on Bonisteel Blvd in front of Chrysler Center. Plan for heavier traffic between 9–10:30 AM on fair days.
