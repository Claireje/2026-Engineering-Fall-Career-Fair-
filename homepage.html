ABOUT THE FAIR

Connecting Tomorrow's Engineers

For four decades, the Fall Engineering Career Fair has been the premier recruiting event connecting top engineering talent with industry-leading companies. Join over 4,000 students and 200+ employers for two days of meaningful connections.
