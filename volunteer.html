WHY VOLUNTEER

Make a Difference. Build Your Network.

Don't miss out on the chance to be part of one of the biggest events of the year. Whether you can spare just an hour or want to join us for the full two days, your help makes a difference. You'll also get to meet members of both Society of Women Engineers and Tau Beta Pi!

No experience needed — just bring your energy and enthusiasm!

Network with Recruiters
Get face time with industry professionals while helping run the fair.
Behind-the-Scenes Experience
See how a 300-company engineering fair comes together from the inside.
Build Leadership Skills
Take ownership of a role and develop skills you'll use throughout your career.
Connect with Students
Meet SWE and TBP members and build relationships that last beyond the fair.
All volunteers get a free t-shirt!

SERVICE HOURS

Your Hours Count

We track all volunteer hours and send them to student organizations after the fair. Members of Tau Beta Pi or those electing will receive service hours counting towards their overall membership requirements, up to 15 hours of service each day.

Organizations that accept Career Fair volunteer hours:

  • Society of Women Engineers
  • Tau Beta Pi
  • Eta Kappa Nu
  • Theta Tau
